Privacy

Privacy

What we collect

If you register: your username, password (hashed, never stored in plaintext), chosen color, and game save data (position, inventory, stats). Guests have no data stored on the server.

Why

To let you save and resume your game across sessions and devices.

Local storage

A session token is stored in your browser's localStorage for automatic login. This is necessary for the game to function. No cookies are used.

Third parties

None. No analytics, no ads, no tracking. Your data is not shared with anyone.

Where

Data is stored on a server in Germany.

Deletion

To request deletion of your account and all associated data, contact dino@d1no.dev.

Under 16

If you are under 16, please play as a guest and do not register an account.
